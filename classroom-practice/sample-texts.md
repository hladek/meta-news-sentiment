# Sample Texts

Use one text per activity, or assign different texts to groups. Each text is short enough to paste directly into the Sentiment Analysis app.

## Text A: School Announcement

The library will remain open for an extra hour during exam week. Students said the quiet space helped them prepare. Some complained that the announcement came too late. The principal said the school will review the schedule earlier next semester.

## Text B: Product Review

The headphones arrived earlier than expected. The sound is clear, and the battery lasted all day. The case feels cheap, and one button is hard to press. I would still recommend them for the price.

## Text C: News-Style Paragraph

The city council approved funding for new bike lanes downtown. Supporters said the plan will make travel safer and reduce traffic. Several business owners warned that construction could hurt sales. The project is scheduled to begin in September.

## Text D: Ambiguous Message

Thanks for submitting the report so quickly. I noticed several sections still need revision. The data table is useful, but the conclusion does not explain why the results matter. Please send an updated version by Friday.

## Text E: Personal Reflection

I was nervous before the presentation. My voice shook during the first minute. After I explained the main idea, the class started asking thoughtful questions. I left feeling more prepared for the next one.

## Text F: Mixed Tone

The team finished the project on time. The final design looked professional. However, several members felt ignored during the planning stage. The teacher praised the result and asked the group to improve collaboration next time.
