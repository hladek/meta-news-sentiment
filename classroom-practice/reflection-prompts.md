# Reflection Prompts

Use these prompts for discussion, exit tickets, learning journals, or small-group reflection.

## Planning

- What clues did I look for before deciding on a sentiment label?
- Did I read the whole text before judging individual sentences?
- Did I separate the sentence's emotional tone from my own reaction to the topic?

## Monitoring

- When did I feel uncertain?
- Did I slow down when a sentence had mixed signals?
- Did I notice any assumptions I was making?

## Evaluating

- Which prediction was strongest? What made it strong?
- Which prediction was weakest? What was missing?
- Did my confidence score match the evidence I had?

## Revising

- What made me change my mind?
- Did the tool reveal a clue I had missed?
- Did I disagree with the tool for a text-based reason or only because the result surprised me?

## Transfer

- How can this process help when reading news, social media posts, essays, or feedback?
- What should a careful reader do before trusting an AI classification?
- What strategy will I use next time I analyze tone?
